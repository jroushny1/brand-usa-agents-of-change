Visit Brand USA Booth for daily AI Sessions and the AI Confessional Happy Hour — hands-on experiences designed for tourism leaders and practitioners exploring how AI is shaping travel marketing. Full session details, times, and registration info below. For full event details, visit ipw.com.

Tuesday, May 19

Leadership Day
10:20 AM

AI Traveler Intelligence

Brand USA embedded an AI trip planner on AmericaTheBeautiful.com — and thousands of international travelers told it exactly where they want to go, who they're traveling with, and what they care about. In this session, Brand USA's Chief AI Officer Janette Roush and Sr. Director of Research & Analytics Chelsea Benitez share first-look findings from a custom analytics pipeline that connects AI conversations to advertising performance — a capability unique in destination marketing. Learn which markets are planning trips the traditional data misses and how AI engagement is rewriting what we know about high-intent visitors.

12:45 PM

AI for Leadership

Your board wants an AI strategy. Your vendors are pitching AI everything. Your team is already using it whether you know it or not. This session cuts through the noise for destination CEOs, CMOs, and senior leaders. Brand USA's Chief AI Officer shares what's working, what failed, and the three investments that actually move the needle for a destination organization.
