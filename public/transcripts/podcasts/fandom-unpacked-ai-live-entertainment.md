# Fandom Unpacked: AI's Impact on Live Entertainment

**Date**: April 23, 2025
**Season**: 1, Episode 9
**Hosts**: Damian Bazadona, Peter Yagecic, Maureen Andersen
**Guest**: Janette Roush, SVP, Innovation and Chief AI Officer at Brand USA

## Episode Description

This episode explores how artificial intelligence is transforming live entertainment discovery and fan experiences. The discussion covers AI-powered recommendations moving away from keyword-driven search toward personalized fan connections, practical tools like ChatGPT Team, Claude, Google Notebook LM, Midjourney, and Descript, and envisions a future where personal AI assistants proactively recommend experiences based on individual preferences and schedules.

## Key Topics Discussed

- Discovery shifts in live entertainment through AI
- Personal AI assistants for entertainment recommendations
- Tools and platforms for AI integration
- Best practices for marketers using AI safely
- Maintaining authenticity and transparency in AI-powered marketing
